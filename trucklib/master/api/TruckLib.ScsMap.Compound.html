Remarks

Both the items and the nodes of compounded items are contained within its compound parent rather than belonging to the sector itself.

The editor does not allow signs to be added to compounds, but signs without traffic rules can be added externally and the game will load them without issues. Likewise, the editor requires a compound to consist of at least two items, but a compound of only one item is supported (albeit somewhat pointless) if created here.
